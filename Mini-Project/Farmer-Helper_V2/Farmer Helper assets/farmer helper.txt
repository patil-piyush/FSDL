Across India, millions of farmers face unpredictable weather, crop diseases, and fluctuating market prices — challenges that directly affect yield and income.
Farmer Helper bridges this gap with AI-driven insights — helping farmers make smarter, faster, and data-backed decisions.



Countries using precision agriculture — like Australia and Israel — have already improved yield efficiency. Farmer Helper brings that same innovation to Indian farms.



Let’s start from the beginning. The landing page welcomes users with a clean overview — from here, farmers can explore features or sign up to get started.



Registering is simple — just name, email, and password. Once logged in, we’re taken straight to the main dashboard.



This is the farmer’s control center — the Dashboard. It shows local weather, market highlights, and recent farm updates. From here, everything’s one click away — disease detection, crop advice, or market insights.



Now, let’s explore crop recommendations. The farmer enters soil pH, N-P-K levels, rainfall, and temperature.
Our trained machine learning model analyzes these inputs and recommends the best crops with confidence scores.


Next, the Disease Detection module. Farmers simply upload a photo of a leaf — the AI model scans the image and instantly detects possible diseases.


Accurate, local weather data matters. Our system uses trusted APIs to show real-time temperature, wind, and rainfall forecasts — personalized to the farmer’s saved location.
Also displays the future forecasting of next two days.


Market Prices gives farmers the power to sell smart. They can search by state, district, commodity and Date — and instantly see current market prices across nearby mandis.


The Profile section stores each farmer’s location, farm size, and preferences.
These details personalize every part of the experience — from local weather to tailored crop recommendations.



Under the hood, our system connects a Node.js backend with Python-based ML services — making predictions and insights fast, reliable, and scalable.


Farmer Helper empowers farmers to make smarter decisions — reducing risks, improving yield, and saving time.
We’ve built a tool that’s simple, actionable, and ready to scale.
